Steps to Prepare a Salad

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: Wash and chop lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
    Peel and slice an avocado.
  2. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
    Add a pinch of dried oregano for flavor.
  3. Assemble the Salad: In a large bowl, combine all the chopped vegetables.
    Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly.
    Add crumbled feta cheese and olives on top.
    Serve immediately.
